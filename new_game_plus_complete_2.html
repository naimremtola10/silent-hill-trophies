⛩️ Shrine Path Puzzles
🎴 Altar Puzzle
💡 Poem:
"Kudzu, respected by all and a leader, was wed to Pine. Plum, loved by all and at the center, was wed to Bamboo. Chrysanthemum, who had no mate, studied under Kudzu and became the Sage of the East."
1. Left: Fan (Bamboo)
2: Branches (Pine - wed to Kudzu)
3. Center: Dress (Plum - center of group)
4: Rat (Kudzu - leader)
5. Right: Pot (Chrysanthemum - Sage of East, no mate)
🔑 Logic: "Wed" = side-by-side. Match keywords to object materials/symbols.
🔒 Shrine Vault Puzzle
⚠️ CRITICAL: Find 3 Yokai Ema. Each has 4 symbols - pick the correct one based on limb count!
📜 Riddle:
"The Yokai of the Carriage had their heads removed. The Yokai of the Umbrella, their arms. The Yokai of the Kettle, their legs."
Step 1: Find 3 Yokai Ema (household objects with demon features)
• Carriage Yokai (2 heads)
• Umbrella Yokai (triangular, 4 arms)
• Kettle Yokai (wearing straw, 3 legs)
Step 2: Count limbs mentioned in riddle:
• Carriage has 2 heads → use 2nd symbol
• Umbrella has 4 arms → use 4th symbol
• Kettle has 3 legs → use 3rd symbol
🔑 Lock Code:
Dial 1 (Carriage): Circle with two vertical lines
Dial 2 (Umbrella): Circle with upside-down Y
Dial 3 (Kettle): Snake with V underneath head
🏆 Trophy: "Untouchable" - Complete without taking damage from blind shackled monster!

🏆 Puppetmaster (Silver) - ENDING 2 BOSS
Boss: Tsukomogami

Trophy Requirement: Get through arrow phase WITHOUT being hit

Arrow Phase:
• 3 arrows in air → dodge when close (3 dodges)
• Volley of quick arrows → multiple fast dodges
• Arrow rain → dodge constantly
• Run forward to boss to end phase

Strategy:
• Equip Suzuran + Boar Omamoris (Perfect Dodge + Stamina)
• OR Shrew Mole (dodges don't cost stamina)
• Waves can hit you (doesn't void trophy)
• Only ARROWS void trophy!
